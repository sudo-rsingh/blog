Escaping the high charges of the brokers

This sections will matter more to the people who might be involved in day trading, options or future trading. Regardless I felt that this is an important thing which normal people who just want to do delivery trade should also be aware of. This majorly stopped when discount brokers were introduced, they did not have such a large staff so they could offer the placing of order with very low charges. SkLearn
